# Day 1 pavement footsteps

`pavement-step-a.ogg`, `pavement-step-b.ogg`, and `pavement-step-c.ogg` are prepared from the Stone variants in **Fantozzi's Footsteps (Grass/Sand & Stone)**, released under CC0.

Source: https://lpc.opengameart.org/content/fantozzis-footsteps-grasssand-stone

The original archive and extracted FLAC/OGG files are retained in `source/`. Runtime variants were resampled to 48 kHz mono, loudness-matched, and given a short anti-click fade.
